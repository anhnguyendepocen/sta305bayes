Presentation

This courses is divided into 3 parts:

  1. Baysian theory
  2. Bayesian computation
  3. Bayesian applications in biomedical sciences

Each part is further divided into 3 or 4 sections, and several videos present the content of the topic in each section.

For each part, a slides deck is provided and split into video capsules where I present the slides. Click on the sections on the left hand side menu to access those.
